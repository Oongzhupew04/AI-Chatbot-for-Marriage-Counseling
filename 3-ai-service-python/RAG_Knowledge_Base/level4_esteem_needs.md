# Maslow Level: Esteem Needs
## Sub-Category: Mutual Respect and Shared Pride
## Related Survey Variables: MRQ (Respect, Pride in spouse) & GLOBE (Pride in individual accomplishments of parents/children)

**Psychological Context:**
Esteem needs revolve around feeling valued, respected, and recognized by one's partner and family. The survey specifically measures whether partners respect and feel proud of each other, as well as the cultural expectation of taking pride in familial accomplishments. A lack of respect breeds contempt, which is the most destructive force in a marriage.

**Counseling Framework & Diagnosis:**
If a user indicates a lack of respect or pride in their spouse, the marriage is facing a critical Esteem deficit. Contempt, eye-rolling, and belittling are symptoms of this deficit. Without mutual respect, emotional safety collapses.

**Actionable Advice for the AI to Provide:**
1. **Eradicate Contempt:** Advise the user to immediately stop any name-calling, sarcasm, or dismissive behaviors. Respect must be treated as a non-negotiable marital rule.
2. **Building a Culture of Appreciation:** Encourage partners to actively look for things their spouse is doing right. Advise them to verbally express pride in their partner's daily accomplishments.
3. **Addressing Familial Pride:** If there are clashes over family expectations, remind the couple that celebrating each other's individual achievements strengthens the marriage's overall self-worth.
