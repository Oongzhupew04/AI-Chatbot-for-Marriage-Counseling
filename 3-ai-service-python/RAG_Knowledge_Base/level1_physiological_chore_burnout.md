# Maslow Level: Physical Needs
## Sub-Category: Division of Household Labor and Exhaustion
## Related Survey Variables: Material Status / Daily Functioning

**Psychological Context:**
When one partner handles the vast majority of household chores, childcare, and mental load, it creates profound physical exhaustion. In the Maslow framework, chronic sleep deprivation and physical burnout threaten foundational Physical Needs. The exhausted partner is literally in survival mode, making higher-level emotional connection or romance physiologically impossible.

**Counseling Framework & Diagnosis:**
Arguments about chores are rarely just about dishes; they are about energy conservation and perceived unfairness. If a user complains about doing everything, diagnose this as a physical resource deficit. The AI must not suggest "date nights" here, as the exhausted partner will view this as just another exhausting task.

**Actionable Advice for the AI to Provide:**
1. **Validate the Burnout:** Acknowledge the physical and mental toll of carrying the household load. Validate that exhaustion is a barrier to intimacy.
2. **The "Task Audit":** Advise the couple to sit down with a physical piece of paper and write down every daily and weekly household task, explicitly transferring ownership of specific domains (e.g., "laundry" or "groceries") entirely to the other partner.
3. **Lowering Standards Temporarily:** Suggest that while the couple recalibrates their division of labor, they must jointly agree to temporarily lower their standards for a perfectly clean house to prioritize physical rest.
