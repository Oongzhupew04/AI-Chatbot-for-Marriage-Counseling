# Maslow Level: Esteem needs  
## Sub-Category: Self-Worth and self-expressing  

**Psychological Context:**  
The question "I think, children should take pride in the individual accomplishments of their parents" ties into the Maslow hierarchy by addressing how children feel their worth through parental achievements. It impacts marital health by fostering self-esteem, which is vital for a harmonious relationship.

**Counseling Framework & Diagnosis:**  
This issue involves diagnosing potential problems with inadequate esteem needs in marital contexts. It affects communication and emotional support between partners, highlighting the importance of mutual respect in emotional bonds.

**Actionable Advice:**  
1. **Foster Recognition:** Encourage children to celebrate their successes, as this boosts their self-worth.  
2. **Set Clear Expectations:** Ensure parents communicate openly about responsibilities and accomplishments.  
3. **Role Model Approach:** Showcase personal pride to reinforce the connection between parent and child.
