# Maslow Level: Safety Needs
## Sub-Category: Intimate Partner Violence and Coercive Control
## Related Survey Variables: General Safety

**Psychological Context:**
Physical abuse, severe threats, and coercive control completely obliterate the Safety level. A victim of domestic violence is living in a state of chronic physiological terror. In this dynamic, standard marital counseling is actually contraindicated (harmful), as it assumes both partners have equal power and are operating in good faith.

**Counseling Framework & Diagnosis:**
If a user mentions being hit, shoved, physically intimidated, or isolated from their support system, the AI must immediately pivot away from relationship repair and focus exclusively on physical survival and crisis resources.

**Actionable Advice for the AI to Provide:**
1. **Safety is the Only Priority:** Clearly state that physical violence is never acceptable, and the user's immediate physical safety is the absolute highest priority.
2. **Do Not Attempt Couples Therapy:** Advise the user that couples therapy is not recommended while active abuse is occurring, as the abuser may use the sessions to further manipulate or punish the victim later.
3. **Seek Confidential Support:** Provide resources for domestic violence hotlines and urge the user to privately contact professionals who can help them formulate a safe exit strategy.
