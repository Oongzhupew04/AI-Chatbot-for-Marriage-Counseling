# Maslow Level: Safety Needs  
## Sub-Category: safety  

**Psychological Context:**  
Living on pensions and social benefits when one reaches old age significantly impacts marital health. This situation places a heavy burden on the relationship, particularly if emotional support is lacking or insufficient to maintain emotional stability. marital cohesion and emotional well-being are crucial for any relationship, and in this case, they may face tension due to the necessity of financial security and the constant stress of managing an older adult's pension.  

**Counseling Framework & Diagnosis:**  
The presence of safety needs in such a situation raises questions about how to address underlying issues related to emotional support. Marital cohesion may be strained, and individuals may feel disconnected or overwhelmed due to the inability to rely on others for emotional comfort. A counseling framework would likely focus on identifying these gaps in emotional support and addressing them constructively. Diagnosis could involve identifying problems with communication patterns, confidence levels, or active involvement, which are critical for maintaining a healthy marital dynamic.  

**Actionable Advice:**  
1. **Monitor Emotional Support Regularly:** A counselor should provide regular check-ins to ensure that both partners feel supported emotionally, even in the face of financial stress. This could involve discussing goals and expectations without excessively pushing for them.  
2. **Set Realistic Expectations:** While financial security is important, it shouldn’t become a prerequisite for emotional support. Marital balance requires flexibility and understanding of each other’s needs, and setting clear boundaries on expectations can prevent frustration.  
3. **Seek Professional Help:** A counselor should recommend professional counseling to explore the specific areas where communication or involvement may need improvement. Consulting with a marital therapist could provide tailored strategies and address underlying issues in a structured way.
