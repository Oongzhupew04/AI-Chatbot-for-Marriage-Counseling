# Maslow Level: Esteem Needs
## Sub-Category: Weaponized Incompetence
## Related Survey Variables: MRQ (Respect) / Daily Functioning

**Psychological Context:**
"Weaponized incompetence" occurs when one partner pretends they are bad at a task (like doing laundry or washing dishes) so the other partner simply gives up and does it for them. This destroys Esteem because it treats the capable partner with profound disrespect, forcing them into a permanent managerial role.

**Counseling Framework & Diagnosis:**
If a user says, "It's just easier if I do it myself," or "My spouse says I'm just better at cleaning," the AI must recognize this as manipulative avoidance. The capable partner feels deeply disrespected and taken advantage of.

**Actionable Advice for the AI to Provide:**
1. **Stop the Rescue:** Advise the capable partner to stop rescuing the spouse. If the spouse does a task poorly, the capable partner must not step in and fix it. They must let the spouse experience the natural consequences of doing it poorly.
2. **Demand Equal Capability:** Firmly state that adult partners are capable of learning basic household skills. Lowering the standard temporarily is necessary while the incompetent partner learns to step up.
3. **Call Out the Pattern:** Encourage the user to gently but directly address the pattern: "When you do a bad job on purpose so I will take over, it makes me feel disrespected and used. I need a partner, not a dependent."
