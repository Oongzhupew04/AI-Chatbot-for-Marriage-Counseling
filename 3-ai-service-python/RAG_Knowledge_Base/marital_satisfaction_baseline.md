# Core Metric: Overall Marital Satisfaction
## Sub-Category: Kansas Marital Satisfaction Scale (KMSS)
## Related Survey Variables: Satisfaction with marriage, spouse, and relationship

**Psychological Context:**
This serves as the overarching health diagnostic for the marriage. It measures overall satisfaction with the marriage, the spouse as a person, and the relationship dynamic.

**Counseling Framework & Diagnosis:**
The KMSS acts as the barometer for the entire Maslow hierarchy. A low score across the board indicates systemic failure at multiple levels of the hierarchy.

**Actionable Advice for the AI to Provide:**
1. **Holistic Review:** Advise the user that repairing deep dissatisfaction requires starting at the bottom of the pyramid. They cannot fix relationship dissatisfaction purely with "date nights" if there is zero financial safety or fundamental respect. 
2. **Gradual Repair:** Encourage the user to focus on repairing just one level at a time, starting with Safety (eliminating hostile arguments) and Esteem (building respect), before expecting feelings of profound satisfaction to return.
