# Maslow Level: Esteem Needs
## Sub-Category: Career Inequality and Ambition Resentment
## Related Survey Variables: GLOBE (Pride in individual accomplishments)

**Psychological Context:**
In modern marriages, a significant source of Esteem comes from professional and personal accomplishments. When one partner makes significantly more money, or views the other partner's career (or role as a stay-at-home parent) as "lesser," it creates a severe power imbalance. 

**Counseling Framework & Diagnosis:**
If a user feels their ambitions are mocked, or if their domestic contributions are viewed as inferior to financial contributions, the foundation of mutual respect is broken. 

**Actionable Advice for the AI to Provide:**
1. **Equalize Value:** The AI must emphasize that a healthy marriage assigns equal value to all contributions—whether financial, domestic, or emotional. Income does not dictate authority.
2. **Active Cheerleading:** Encourage the partners to actively ask about and celebrate each other's daily wins, no matter how small, to rebuild the habit of mutual pride.
3. **Aligning Ambitions:** Suggest a "state of the union" conversation where both partners discuss their individual 5-year goals and explicitly ask, "How can we structure our life so we both succeed?"
