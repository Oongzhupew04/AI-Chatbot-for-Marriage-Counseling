# Maslow Level: Physical Needs
## Sub-Category: Material and Financial Resources
## Related Survey Variables: Material Status 

**Psychological Context:**
In the context of this counseling framework, "Material Status" directly reflects Maslow's Physical Needs. It evaluates whether a couple's material situation is much better, similar, or worse than average. Scarcity at this foundational level creates chronic physiological stress, shifting the brain into survival mode, which often triggers constant arguments about money, housing, and basic provisions.

**Counseling Framework & Diagnosis:**
When a user reports severe dissatisfaction with their material or financial status (a score of 4 or 5 indicating worse/much worse than average), recognize this as a foundational deficit. A marriage cannot focus on self-actualization or high-level romance if survival needs are threatened.

**Actionable Advice for the AI to Provide:**
1. **Validation:** Acknowledge the immense stress that material and financial scarcity puts on a marriage. Normalize their anxiety.
2. **Teamwork Orientation:** Advise the couple to approach their material reality as "us against the problem" rather than blaming one another for the scarcity.
3. **Actionable Step:** Suggest foundational resource management—creating a strict survival budget together, finding local community resources if necessary, and prioritizing basic household stability before tackling higher-level emotional complex issues.
